About Us

The Flavour Route was founded in 2022 by culinary explorer and visionary Swatick Nandy, with one mission: to make every journey through taste and hospitality unforgettable. What began as a passionate dream has grown into a national experience that celebrates India’s rich culture—served on a plate and wrapped in comfort.

    ✨ Today, The Flavour Route spans across India, with:

  • Over 50+ unique restaurants serving regionally inspired cuisine
  • More than 150+ luxurious hotels and boutique stays
  • A family of 20,000+ dedicated employees delivering flavour and warmth every single day

    • 🍛 Our culinary footprint includes:

  • Authentic regional kitchens from Kashmir to Kanyakumari
  • Signature tasting menus curated by award-winning chefs
  • Fusion cafés and heritage bistros that blend tradition with innovatio
  • Rooftop dining, poolside brunches, and destination-based feasts

    • 🏨 Our hospitality offerings include:

  • Boutique hotels with curated interiors and locally inspired designs
  • Private stays, penthouses, and retreat villas
  • Bespoke experiences like wellness dining, midnight food trails, and chef’s table journeys

👥 We proudly collaborate with 20,000+ local culinary experts, hosts, and hospitality professionals who understand not just how to serve food—but how to serve memories.

🚀 Under the creative leadership of Founder, CEO & CMO – Swatick Nandy, The Flavour Route continues to revolutionize food and stay experiences with digital menus, region-based recommendations, and seamless booking across platforms.

🎯 Our vision is to become India’s most celebrated lifestyle hospitality brand—where taste meets travel, and rest meets richness. The Flavour Route is not just a brand—it’s a soulful journey of flavour, comfort, and discovery. Come with an appetite. Stay with a smile. Leave with a story.
