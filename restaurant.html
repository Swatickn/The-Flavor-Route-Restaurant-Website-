Floor & Lounges

    Velvet Ember Lounge -


    Warm, luxurious, and perfect for evening cocktails and soft jazz.


    Theme- Luxe and moody, with dark velvets, brass accents, and glowing candlelight. Live jazz or soft blues in the background.

    Signature Drink-- Crimson Smoke — pomegranate, clove-infused syrup, bourbon (or pomegranate-cinnamon mocktail) served over smoked ice.

    Tagline- “India’s spice trail, reimagined.”



    The Spice Deck -


    A rooftop or terrace lounge inspired by India’s rich spice heritage.


    Theme-Open-air terrace with warm-toned woods, hanging spice baskets, and terracotta murals. Perfect for dusk hangouts.

    Signature Drink -- Masala Mojito — muddled mint, lime, roasted cumin, and soda with a dash of tamarind.

    Tagline-“India’s spice trail, reimagined.”



    Saffron & Smoke -


    Earthy, aromatic, and ideal for a fusion tapas and grill concept.


    Theme -Earthy textures, open grills, and golden saffron hues. Artisanal tapas and smoke-plated entrees take center stage.

    Signature Drink -- Sunset Saffron Fizz — saffron, cardamom, orange zest, and tonic with a hint of smoke.

    TagLine -- “Where tradition is fired to life.”



    Route 22 Lounge -


    A nod to your founding year (2022) and the idea of a flavorful journey.


    Theme -Travel-inspired with vintage maps, leather booths, and a mini “passport menu” featuring drinks from Indian regions.

    Signature Drink -- - Traveler’s Tonic — kokum, lemongrass, Himalayan salt, and gin or tonic water

    TagLine -- “Tasting India, mile by mile.”



    The Banyan Bar -


    Rooted in Indian tradition, with a modern twist—great for a garden or courtyard setting.


    Theme - Garden-lounge meets forest café—natural wood, ambient green lighting, and banyan-inspired wall art. Very Zen.

    Signature Drink -Neem & Nectar — tulsi, jaggery, citrus, muddled with herbal bitters.

    TagLine -- “Rooted in calm. Poured with soul.”



    Noor Lounge -


    Meaning “light” in Arabic and Urdu, perfect for a serene, ambient space with soft lighting and soulful music.


    Theme - Mystical, ambient space with lattice lights, soft gold upholstery, and Sufi instrumental music by night.

    Signature Drink - - Moon Mist — rose water, lychee, elderflower, topped with a mist of edible shimmer.

    TagLine -- “A glow beyond the glass.”
